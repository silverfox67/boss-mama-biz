Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Do I need a huge social media following to make money?
Absolutely not. Digital products and affiliate marketing work even with a small or zero following. The systems Kristan recommends focus on automated funnels, not viral content. You don't need 100,000 followers — you need the right offer in front of the right person.
Is this another MLM or pyramid scheme?
100% no. You are not recruiting anyone. You are not required to hit monthly purchase quotas. Affiliate marketing means you recommend a product, someone buys it, you earn a commission. No downlines. No monthly minimums. No pressure on your friends and family.
What is PLR and how does it work?
PLR (Private Label Rights) means you purchase a done-for-you digital product — like an ebook, template, or course — and you get the right to rebrand it as your own and resell it for 100% of the profit. You didn't create it, but you own the right to sell it. Use our free PLR Filter tool to find the right products for your niche.
Why use this site instead of just going to Stan Store directly?
Stan Store is just a link page. This site is a full business toolkit — an interactive income quiz, a potential calculator, a PLR filter, an AI income coach, and curated recommendations with full breakdowns. You get context, clarity, and Kristan's personal endorsement before you spend a single dollar.
How much time does this realistically take per day?
The whole point is to minimize your active time. Once your funnel is set up, it collects leads and makes sales without you needing to be online. Most of the systems recommended here are designed to require less than 1-2 hours per day once running — and some near zero once fully automated.
I'm not techy at all. Will this work for me?
Yes. Every offer on this site was selected partly because it's beginner-friendly. Stacked by Emily literally shows you the screen and walks you through it. The $27 Create & Sell guide is designed for someone who has never made a digital product before. If you can use your phone, you can do this.
